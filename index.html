About Us

Berker Group AS a privately held Norwegian investment company that was founded in 2012. The company is based in Oslo, Norway, and invest in entrepreneurial talent, either commercial or arts. To this date we have invested in more than 40 startups globally. We are also a limited partner in 5 early stage funds, and over 10 contemporary artists.
